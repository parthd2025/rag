# Remote Work Policy - Updated

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2024

---

## 1. Remote Work Policy Overview

This policy outlines guidelines for employees working remotely. All remote work arrangements must be approved by the employee's direct manager and department head.

---

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Eligible Positions
- Software engineers and developers
- Data analysts
- Product managers
- Marketing specialists
- Finance analysts
- Business development roles

### 2.2 Ineligible Positions
- Reception and customer service
- HR operations
- Facilities management
- Any role requiring on-site presence

---

## 3. Remote Work Arrangements

### 3.1 Hybrid Model
- **Minimum office days:** 2 days per week
- **Flexible remote:** 3 days per week from home
- **Office location:** San Francisco HQ or approved satellite offices

### 3.2 Full Remote
- Available only with executive approval
- Limited to specific departments
- Must maintain regular communication

---

## 4. Work Hours and Availability

### 4.1 Core Hours
- **Required:** 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Pacific Time
- Flexible start times before 10:00 AM
- Must attend all scheduled meetings

### 4.2 Communication
- Daily standup meetings (10:30 AM)
- Weekly team syncs (scheduled by manager)
- Respond to messages within 2 hours

---

## 5. Remote Work Setup

### 5.1 Home Office Requirements
- Quiet, professional work environment
- High-speed internet (minimum 10 Mbps)
- Secure Wi-Fi with password protection
- Dedicated desk or workspace

### 5.2 Equipment Provided
- Laptop and monitor
- Keyboard and mouse
- Company phone
- Video camera and headset

### 5.3 Technical Support
- IT support available 8 AM - 6 PM
- VPN access required for all company systems
- 2-factor authentication mandatory

---

## 6. Communication Standards

### 6.1 Video Conferencing
- Camera on during all team meetings
- Professional appearance expected
- Neutral background or blur feature

### 6.2 Meeting Expectations
- Punctuality is critical
- Prepare in advance
- Active participation required

---

## 7. Productivity and Performance

### 7.1 Monitoring
- Activity monitoring software may be used
- Screenshot captures every 30 minutes
- Tracks application usage

**NOTE: This contradicts the handbook privacy expectations**

### 7.2 Performance Metrics
- Same expectations as office staff
- Deliverables and deadlines unchanged
- Quality standards maintained

---

## 8. Termination of Remote Status

Remote work arrangements can be terminated with:
- 2 weeks notice by company
- 1 week notice by employee
- Immediate termination for policy violations

---

## 9. Vacation and Holidays

### 9.1 Leave While Remote
- Leave policy remains unchanged
- Annual leave: 15-25 days based on tenure
- All leave must be approved in advance

**ISSUE: Contradicts handbook on leave application timing**

---

## 10. Equipment and Expenses

### 10.1 Company-Provided Equipment
- All costs covered by company
- Employees responsible for equipment security
- Return required upon termination

### 10.2 Home Office Allowance
- $500 one-time setup stipend
- Monthly internet allowance: $50
- Office supplies: $100 per year

**NOTE: Not mentioned in main handbook**

---

## 11. Data Security

### 11.1 Compliance Requirements
- No use of personal devices for work
- All files encrypted and backed up
- Report security incidents immediately
- Annual security training required

### 11.2 Confidentiality
- Same as office employees
- Non-disclosure agreement applies
- Client information must be protected
