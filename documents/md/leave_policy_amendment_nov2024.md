# Leave Policy Amendment - November 2024

**Amendment Date:** November 15, 2024
**Effective Date:** December 1, 2024

---

## 1. Overview

This amendment updates and modifies the leave policies outlined in the Employee Handbook 2024. All changes are effective immediately for new leave applications.

---

## 2. Annual Leave Updates

### 2.1 New Annual Leave Entitlement

**Previous Policy (Handbook 2024):**
- Year 1-2: 15 days per year
- Year 3-5: 20 days per year
- Year 6+: 25 days per year

**UPDATED POLICY (Effective Dec 1, 2024):**
- Year 1: 18 days per year (+3 days)
- Year 2-3: 22 days per year (+2 days)
- Year 4-6: 25 days per year (no change)
- Year 7+: 28 days per year (+3 days)

### 2.2 Accrual and Carryover
- Annual leave accrues monthly
- Maximum carryover: 10 days to next year
- Unused leave beyond 10 days will be forfeited
- Exceptions available for documented business reasons

---

## 3. Sick Leave Modifications

### 3.1 Updated Sick Leave Entitlement

**Previous:** 10 days per year (non-accumulating)

**UPDATED:** 12 days per year (non-accumulating)

### 3.2 Medical Certificate Requirements
- Required after 2 consecutive days (updated from 3)
- Doctor's note must include diagnosis (general format acceptable)
- Telemedicine consultations now accepted with documentation

### 3.3 Emergency Leave
- Up to 3 days paid emergency leave per year
- Manager approval required (or HR if manager unavailable)
- Medical or family emergency purposes only

---

## 4. Parental Leave Policy (NEW)

### 4.1 Maternity Leave
- 16 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: 12 weeks paid leave
- Additional 8 weeks unpaid available

### 4.2 Paternity Leave
- 4 weeks paid leave
- Same adoption benefits
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth

### 4.3 Shared Parental Leave
- Parents may share up to 20 weeks
- Both must work for the company
- Each parent minimum 2 weeks

---

## 5. Bereavement Leave (NEW)

### 5.1 Leave Entitlement
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days paid
- Extended family (grandparent, in-laws): 3 days paid
- Multiple bereavements: Up to 10 days per year

### 5.2 Application Process
- Notify manager as soon as possible
- Bereavement leave is automatic (no approval needed)
- Death certificate or obituary may be requested

---

## 6. Sabbatical Leave (NEW)

### 6.1 Eligibility
- Employees with 5+ years service
- Available once every 10 years
- Unpaid leave option

### 6.2 Duration and Conditions
- Duration: 4-8 weeks
- Must provide 12 weeks notice
- Performance must be satisfactory
- Job position held upon return

---

## 7. Study Leave (NEW)

### 7.1 Entitlement
- 5 days per year for job-related training
- Additional 10 days per year for professional certifications
- Paid leave if approved by department

### 7.2 Requirements
- Course must align with job role or company needs
- Advance notice required
- Pass completion required to qualify for future study leave

---

## 8. Leave Application Process Update

### 8.1 Advanced Notice Requirements

**Updated Requirements:**
- Annual leave: Minimum 3 weeks advance notice (updated from 2 weeks)
- Sick leave: Notify manager on the day
- Emergency leave: Notify within 24 hours
- Study leave: 4 weeks advance notice

### 8.2 Approval Authority
- Manager: Annual leave up to 5 days
- Department Head: Annual leave 6-10 days
- HR Director: Annual leave exceeding 10 days

---

## 9. Leave During Public Holidays

### 9.1 Public Holiday Rules
- Employees not required to work public holidays
- If required to work: Double pay or lieu day off
- Public holiday falls on weekend: No compensation

### 9.2 Work-Required Holidays
- Company may require work on specific holidays
- Notice must be given 2 weeks in advance
- Employee may refuse with subject to compensation policy

---

## 10. Leave Documentation

### 10.1 Required Documentation
- Leave application form (updated version required)
- Reason for leave (mandatory field)
- Backup contact for urgent matters
- Return date and first day back confirmation

### 10.2 Medical Documentation
- Doctor's note for leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Telemedicine reports accepted
- Private medical information not required

---

## 11. Leave Payment Upon Termination

### 11.1 Final Payment
- All accrued annual leave paid out
- Sick leave: Not paid out (non-accumulating)
- Emergency leave: Not paid out if unused
- Calculation: Daily rate Ã— days accrued

### 11.2 Notice Period Leave
- Leave during notice period counted toward final payment
- Manager can request work during notice period
- Employee entitled to use accrued leave during notice

---

## 12. Conflicting Handbook Provisions

The following handbook provisions are superseded by this amendment:

- **Section 4.1** - Annual leave entitlements (old rates no longer apply)
- **Section 4.2** - Sick leave medical certificate threshold (now 2 days not 3)
- **Section 4.3** - Leave application notice period (now 3 weeks not 2)
