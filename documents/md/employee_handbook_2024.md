## 2. Employment Policies

### 2.1 Work Schedule
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Lunch break: 1 hour (unpaid), typically 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM
- Flexibility: Subject to department requirements and manager approval

### 2.2 Office Attendance
All employees are required to be present in the office unless special arrangements have been made with their manager.

### 2.3 Dress Code
Business casual attire is required. This includes:
- Collared shirts or blouses
- Dress pants or skirts
- Closed-toe shoes
- No denim or athletic wear

---

## 3. Employee Loan Policy

**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department

### 3.1 Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum 2 years of continuous service
- Full-time employees only
- Department managers must approve requests

### 3.2 Types of Eligible Employees
- Permanent full-time employees
- Contract employees with 2+ years tenure
- Ineligible: Temporary staff, part-time employees, consultants

### 3.3 Loan Application Process
1. Submit completed application to HR
2. Manager approval required
3. HR review and verification
4. Finance department approval
5. Employee receives loan agreement to sign

### 3.4 Loan Repayment
- Standard term: 12-36 months
- Deduction through payroll
- Interest rate: 2% annually
- Early repayment allowed without penalty

---

## 4. Leave Policy

### 4.1 Annual Leave Entitlement
- Year 1-2: 15 days per year
- Year 3-5: 20 days per year
- Year 6+: 25 days per year

### 4.2 Sick Leave
- 10 days per year
- Medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days
- Non-accumulating (does not roll over)

### 4.3 Leave Application
- Minimum 2 weeks advance notice required
- Submit request to direct manager
- Manager approval needed before booking travel

---

## 5. Code of Conduct

### 5.1 Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Maintain professional communication
- Prohibits discrimination and harassment
- Zero tolerance for violence or threats

### 5.2 Confidentiality
- All company information is confidential
- Non-disclosure agreement applies
- Breach may result in legal action

---

## 6. Health and Safety

### 6.1 Workplace Safety
- Report all hazards to manager
- Follow all safety procedures
- Participate in safety training
- Emergency procedures must be understood

### 6.2 Health Insurance
- Company covers 80% of premium
- Coverage begins first day of employment
- Spouse and dependent coverage available
- Annual enrollment in October

---

## 7. Performance Management

### 7.1 Performance Reviews
- Annual review in December
- Mid-year check-in in June
- 360-degree feedback for managers
- Promotion based on performance and capability

### 7.2 Evaluation Criteria
- Quality of work
- Timeliness of deliverables
- Team collaboration
- Professional development
- Communication skills

---

## 8. Disciplinary Process

### 8.1 Disciplinary Levels
1. **Verbal Warning** - Minor infractions
2. **Written Warning** - Repeated offenses
3. **Suspension** - Serious violations
4. **Termination** - Final step

### 8.2 Appeals Process
Employees have 5 days to appeal any disciplinary action to HR.

---

## 9. Termination Policy

### 9.1 Notice Periods
- By Employee: 2 weeks for non-management, 4 weeks for management
- By Company: 2 weeks severance pay minimum
- Cause termination: No notice required

### 9.2 Final Paycheck
- All accrued leave paid out
- Final paycheck issued within 5 business days
- Company equipment must be returned
